# Affiliate Programs (Shortlist)

## Trip.com Affiliate
- Commission examples: hotels ~6%, flights ~0.8% (tiered)
- Cookie: 30d web, 7d app (per FAQ)
- Payout: monthly settlement; 40–60 working days after
- Sources: https://www.trip.com/partners/help/faq | https://www.trip.com/partners

## Agoda Affiliate
- Commission: up to 7%
- Requires website (unless travel agent with docs)
- Payout: month after stay; min payout $200
- Sources: https://partners.agoda.com/en-us/ | https://partners.agoda.com/en-us/faq.html

## Expedia Group Travel Creator
- Payout after trip completion; monthly processing; 60–150 days typical
- Minimum threshold: £20 / $30 / €30 per currency
- Sources: https://creator.expediagroup.com/affiliates | https://help.creator.expediagroup.com/hc/en-us

## Notes
- Use affiliate links for booking; never claim to issue tickets.
- Always include disclosure in alerts and landing pages.
